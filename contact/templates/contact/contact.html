FAQs

All the prints available to purchase are printed onto aluminium using the latest high-tech methods. The prints will not appear shiny and have a matt appearance. We only provide the best quality products and if you are not happy with your purchase, please send us an e-mail to arrange a refund or exchange.
All the prints come with two magnetic pads for the two top corners. These can be stuck to walls and should not be damaging to upholstery unless left for more than 2 years. The magnetic pads can be placed at the best place for your surfaces, however we advise you use the pads on the top two corners for maximising the security of the fastenings.
Delivery is based at rates for the UK only. Shipping to overseas destinations will unfortunately cost more. Once confirmed, the order should take 5 - 7 working days to be delivered using our courier service. If you are unhappy with your purchase, please e-mail to discuss any returns or refunds.
We hope that you enjoy your purchase, however sometimes what can look nice on a computer screen can be wrong for our homes or offices, or maybe the wrong size was ordered. No worries, just send us an e-mail from the form above to begin a discussion about returning, refunding or exchanging any prints you are unhappy with. Returns postage is not covered by Picturesque, but exchange deliveries will be free of charge.
